Extracurriculars

Coding Club

September 2019 – Present

In Coding Club, we learn new programming languages and participate in hackathons and programming contests.

Concert Band

September 2016 – Present

In Concert Band, I play the alto saxophone and have previously played percussion instruments. Rehearsing semiweekly, we play seasonal concerts and during school night events, such as Arts Night. We also participate in the annual Capital Region Music Festival competition, which brings in schools across Easten Ontario and Western Quebec.

Environmental Council

September 2019 – Present

The A.Y. Jackson Environmental Council aims to reduce the school's overall environmental impact through the implementation of new policies, such as the introduction of green bins for compostable waste, as well as the emphasis of our daily impacts on the environment.

@ayj_eco

Health Science Club

September 2016 – Present

In Health Science Club, we organize and run health-science-related events, such as an event to obtain a first-aid certification and seminars, all of which lead up to the HOSA competition.

Jazz Band

September 2017 – Present

In Jazz Band, I played the baritone saxophone from September 2017 to June 2018, and currently play the alto saxophone. Rehearsing semiweekly, we play seasonal concerts and during school night events, such as Arts Night.

ManUp

September 2016 – June 2017

In ManUp, we collaborated with other schools and clubs to raise awareness to help end sexual violence against both men and women, as well as promoting safe dating practices, through events such as seminars and school assemblies.

Me to We Club

September 2016 – Present

In Me to We Club, we collaborate with local organizations and other school clubs to plan, advertise, organize, and run school and community events to raise funds for WE initiatives. These initiatives include providing education, clean water, medical care, and more for citizens in third-world countries.

MEDLIFE

September 2019 – Present

Similarly to Me to We Club, we collaborate with local organizations and other school clubs to plan, advertise, organize, and run school and community events to raise funds for the MEDLIFE Fund, which aims to deliver community development projects, such as hygiene and school projects, to Ecuador, Peru and Tanzania.

Outers Club

October 2019 – Present

Outers Club focuses on outdoor activities throughout the year, such as hiking and exploring caves, and cycling.
